What to do next?

If you would like to join us you are welcome to come along for up to three training sessions to see if what we offer is right for you.

Joining a new club can be a bit daunting so we are keen to give all potential members a good opportunity to see how we work so that at the end of the free trial period you can then make an informed decision.

If you choose to join, we offer a simple monthly subscription arrangement to all full members. This is payable over a twelve month period. Your monthly subscription allows you to attend all the available coached training sessions that month AND includes your annual payment to Swim England in the “club train” category.

If you are interested in entering competitive swim galas, your membership will need to be in the “club compete” category. For this we ask for a single additional payment in the January of each year.

The cost of the monthly subscription is identified by the club trustees /committee members and is ratified each year at the Annual General Meeting.

For details of the current subscription - please contact the club.

We are always pleased to consider temporary membership arrangements, e.g. students at home who might want to continue training during the holidays and we are willing to identify appropriate payments for such arrangements.

As a club and as a registered charity we are interested in people having the opportunity to swim. We recognise that there can be times when finances may be very stretched and we would urge any member who might be having difficulties to talk to the club welfare lead.
