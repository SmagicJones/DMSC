DMSC - Open Water

Open Water

Many club members enjoy open water swimming and take part in a wide range of events during the year. The club does not organise open water events but there are members who can offer advice and support if this is an area of swimming new to you.

Good information about open water swimming can be found in the members handbook.

Similarly, many members of the club take part in triathlon events - from international iron man events to local triathlons. We appreciate that no one will win a triathlon on the strength of their swim alone but for a good many club members , the chance to be swim-fit and technically competent means finishing the swim leg in good shape for the bike and run stages to come.
