Welcome to Darwen Masters Swimming Club and thank you for your interest in the club.

Established in 1988, Darwen Masters has been providing high quality structured swimming opportunities for adults from Darwen and the surrounding areas for over 30 years.

The club is proud to be based at Darwen Leisure Centre - located in the heart of Darwen. The club currently has two dedicated training sessions a week
