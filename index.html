Welcome to Darwen Masters Swimming Club

Find Out More

Welcome to Darwen Masters Swimming Club and thank you for your interest in the club.

Established in 1988, Darwen Masters has been providing high quality structured swimming opportunities for adults from Darwen and the surrounding areas for over 30 years.

The club is proud to be based at Darwen Leisure Centre - located in the heart of Darwen. The club currently has two dedicated training sessions a week

Wednesday Evening

8 till 9 pm

Saturday Afternoon

4 till 5 pm

The club caters for swimmers with a range of abilities - so whether swimming is important to you for health and wellbeing or you want to enter masters swimming galas ,improve your swimming as part of triathlon training or enjoy open water swimming and events - Darwen Masters is the place for you.

The following sections will tell you a bit more about the club and how we work. The club aims to provide a friendly, supportive and positively challenging opportunity for you to develop your swimming in ways that meet your personal goals.

We aim to be an inclusive club with flexibility to accommodate individual needs.

If you have any questions, please do get in touch using the contact form.

Get Involved!

DMSC - Membership

If you are thinking about joining us - great - you will be welcome to come along for up to three training sessions to see if what we offer is right for you.

We know that joining a new club can be a bit daunting so we are keen to give all potential members a good opportunity to see how we work.

At the end of the trial period you can then make an informed decision about joining the club.

The trial period is free of charge.

If you choose to join, we offer a simple monthly subscription arrangement to all full members. This is payable over the twelve month period. Your monthly subscription allows you to attend all the available coached training sessions that month AND includes your annual payment to Swim England in the “club train” category.

If you are interested in entering competitive swim galas - your membership will need to be in the “club compete” category. For this we ask for a single additional payment in the January of each year.

As a club our financial aim is to break even and the prices we charge reflect that aim.

The cost of the monthly subscription is identified by the club trustees /committee members and is ratified each each year at the Annual General Meeting.

For details of the current subscription - please contact the club.

We are always pleased to consider temporary membership arrangements - eg students at home who might want to continue training during the holidays - and are willing to identify appropriate payments for such arrangements.

As a club and as a registered charity we are interested in people having the opportunity to swim. We recognise that there can be times when finances may be very stretched. We would urge any member who might be having difficulties to talk to the club welfare lead.
